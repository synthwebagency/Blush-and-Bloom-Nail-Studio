Our Story

Meet Marwa Smith

Blush & Bloom was born from Marwa Smith's lifelong passion for beauty and artistic expression. As a professional nail artist with over a decade of experience in the high-end beauty industry, Marwa envisioned a space where luxury meets genuine care.

"I wanted to create more than just a nail salon," says Marwa. "I wanted a sanctuary where every woman feels seen, celebrated, and pampered. Our studio is built on the pillars of absolute cleanliness, premium quality, and an unwavering attention to detail."
